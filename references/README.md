## Rules

- Do not commit copyrighted screenshots from commercial games, commercial
  maps, social media, official press photos, or tourism pages unless the
  page/file license explicitly allows reuse.
- Use self-rendered references, public-domain media, CC0 media, or
  attribution-compatible Wikimedia/Commons files with preserved metadata.
- Keep geometry anchored to Berlin LoD2 and OSM; references here are for
  material, colour, facade and texture cues only.
- Keep attribution visible in public artefacts when reference-derived
  colours or textures are used.

## Target Look

- late-1990s SimCity / RollerCoaster Tycoon legibility;
- Berlin government-quarter architecture: glass, pale stone, metal, roof
  ribs, bridge structures and Spree edges;
- Tiergarten vegetation: layered trees, shrubs, path texture, pond and
  water-ripple cues;
- small landmark cues that help orientation without replacing measured
  source geometry.
